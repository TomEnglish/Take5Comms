Email Notification Setup

Branded email alerts for your Take 5 Close-Out App

The app sends two automated emails daily — a nightly compliance summary (10 PM) and an overdue deposit alert (2 PM). Right now these come from a generic address. To make them come from alerts@take5ventures.com, we need to verify your domain. This is a one-time setup.
Why this matters: Email providers (Gmail, Outlook) require domain verification to prevent spoofing. Without it, emails from your domain will be rejected or land in spam. This is the same process used by any email marketing or notification service.

1 Who needs to do this?

Whoever manages the DNS records for take5ventures.com. This is typically your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.) or your IT person. If you're not sure, check where you bought the domain.
