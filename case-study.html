The Client

Take 5 Oil Change operates 16 quick-lube locations across three markets in the southeastern U.S. (CSRA Georgia, Western North Carolina, and Tri-Cities Tennessee). Each store processes $3,000–$8,000 in daily transactions across 3–4 credit card terminals, plus cash deposits.

The Challenge

Before

  • Nightly close-outs done on paper — no central visibility
  • Cash deposit tracking relied on manual spreadsheets
  • Variances discovered days or weeks after they occurred
  • No automated compliance monitoring across 16 stores
  • Office staff had no real-time view of which stores had submitted

After

  • Managers photograph receipts — AI extracts all financial data
  • Real-time reconciliation with instant variance detection
  • Automated nightly compliance emails (who submitted, who didn't)
  • Overdue deposit alerts when cash sits undeposited >48 hours
  • Web dashboard with full visibility across all stores and markets

What Was Built

Mobile App (iOS + Android) — Store managers snap photos of their POS close-out report, batch settlement receipts, and deposit slips. AI-powered OCR extracts financial data with 100% accuracy on real thermal paper. The app calculates variance in real-time, flags discrepancies over $15, and supports offline submission with automatic sync.

Web Dashboard — Office staff and administrators see a compliance grid across all 16 stores, track overdue deposits, analyze variance trends over time, review individual submissions with photo galleries, and export data to CSV for accounting.

Automated Monitoring — Two daily email alerts run automatically: a 10 PM nightly compliance summary showing which stores submitted and which didn't, and a 2 PM deposit follow-up flagging any cash that hasn't been deposited within 48 hours.

Infrastructure — Cloud-hosted PostgreSQL database with row-level security (managers see only their store, office sees everything), nightly automated backups, photo storage, and four serverless edge functions handling OCR, email, and compliance checks.

5-Day Build Timeline

Day 1 — Foundation
Database schema, auth system, store seed data, mobile app scaffolding with Expo/React Native, submission wizard with 3-step flow
Day 2 — OCR & Core Flow
Multi-pass AI OCR pipeline (Haiku extraction → math validation → Sonnet fallback), auto-fill from receipt photos, reconciliation calculations, variance flagging
Day 3 — Dashboard & Email
Web dashboard (Next.js), compliance grid, deposit tracker, variance trends, CSV export, Resend email integration, cron job scheduling
Day 4 — Production & Testing
Migration to hosted Supabase, 34 user accounts created, real receipt testing (100% OCR accuracy), EAS builds for iOS/Android, nightly backup pipeline via GitHub Actions
Day 5 — Polish & Delivery
UX fixes (keyboard handling, date validation, duplicate guards), soft-delete resubmit flow, demo videos, client deliverables documentation, DNS email setup
