Testing Checklist: Store Manager

Have a manager at a test store run through these steps on their phone.

NIGHTLY CLOSE-OUT
  1. Open the app and sign in with your email and password
  2. Select yesterday's date (or today if the store is already closed) and tap Nightly Close
  3. Take a photo of the POS close-out report
    The app will try to read the numbers automatically (OCR). You can correct anything it gets wrong.
  4. Take photos of each batch receipt (one per terminal)
    Enter batch totals, tips, and record counts if they weren't picked up by OCR.
  5. Enter the cash counted at close
    The app will show whether the cash matches the expected amount.
  6. If the variance exceeds $15, select a reason: Refund or Other
    Refund requires no further explanation. Other asks for a brief note (at least 10 characters).
  7. Review the summary and tap Submit
DEPOSIT SLIP (next day)
  1. Open the app and select the same date as the nightly close-out
  2. Tap Deposit Slip
  3. Take a photo of the bank deposit receipt
  4. Enter the deposit amount and the date the deposit was made
  5. Submit

Testing Checklist: Office / Admin

After a manager submits, check these on the web dashboard.

DASHBOARD REVIEW
  1. Log in at t5dashboard.netlify.app
  2. Check the Dashboard — the test store should show green (submitted)
  3. Click into the submission to see receipt photos, extracted numbers, and reconciliation math
  4. Check the Flagged Variances page if the submission had a variance over $15
  5. Check the Deposit Tracker to see outstanding deposits
  6. Go to Store Management — try adding a test user, assigning them to a store, then removing them

COMPLIANCE & ALERTS

  • 2:00 PM ET — Deposit follow-up email if any stores haven't deposited cash within 72 hours of their close-out
These emails go to the configured office email address. Let us know the email address where you'd like to receive these alerts.

What to Look For

As you test, keep an eye on these questions:

  • Does the form flow make sense for your managers? Is anything confusing?
  • Are the OCR results accurate on your receipt formats?
  • Is anything missing that should be there?
  • Are there fields that should be required but aren't (or vice versa)?
  • Does the dashboard give you the information you need at a glance?

Don't worry about breaking anything — that's what testing is for. Just note what works, what doesn't, and what you'd change.
